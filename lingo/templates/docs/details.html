Welcome to Lingo Cafe!

Lingo Cafe is an open community of people from all over the world practising language and socializing.

We meet over online video conference calls. Sessions last 2 hours and host up to 100 participants. Conversations take place in smaller groups of usually up to 5 people.

Each meeting has one topic language. Participants are expected to have at least a very basic level of understanding the session language.

During the sessions, hosts guide the participants by facilitating conversations and assisting them during their practices.

Anyone having fluency in the language of the session can volunteer to be a co-host. Please indicate your intention to co-host a meeting when you are signing up. If it is your first time, an experienced host will guide you through the process.

Before participating in any activity, please read and make sure you fully agree with the community code of conduct.
